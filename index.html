The loop

Four steps. No screenshots, no re-pastes. click → note → revise → done.

  1. 1

    Agent writes HTML

    A real page lands in your browser. The agent's primitives are the same ones your finished product will use.

  2. 2

    Click what's wrong

    You scan the page. Spot what's off. Click it — outline goes terracotta, comment form opens. Type a note.

  3. 3

    Revise just that part

    Not the whole page. Just the element. The note stays attached; resolve or reply.

  4. 4

    Ship it

    Say ship it. The agent hands you clean HTML — no rail, no chrome.

    Shipped
The whole point

The document is the conversation.

No screenshots, no re-pastes, no re-reading. The thing you're arguing about is the thing itself — and you can click on it.
